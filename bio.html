Bio and CV

Education

He was born in Lamongan, East Java, Indonesia, and completed his elementary, middle, and high school education in his hometown. After that, he continued his higher education at the Department of Mathematics at Institut Teknologi Sepuluh Nopember (ITS) in Surabaya, Indonesia and received Bachelor, Master, and Doctoral degrees in Applied Mathematics in 2015, 2018, and 2024, respectively.

Work

In August-September 2014 and March-June 2015, he conducted an internship program at the Avionics at Aerospace Technology Center, National Institute of Aeronautics and Space (LAPAN), Bogor, Indonesia. Since February 2022, he has been part of the Department of Electrical Engineering at Universitas Islam Lamongan (UNISLA) in Lamongan, Indonesia. The department was initially part of the Faculty of Engineering, which has since been reorganized as the Faculty of Science and Technology (FST).
