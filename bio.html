Biography

Education

He was born in Lamongan, East Java, Indonesia, where he also completed his elementary, middle, and high school education. He then pursued higher education at the Department of Mathematics, Institut Teknologi Sepuluh Nopember (ITS), in Surabaya, Indonesia, earning his Bachelor's, Master's, and Doctoral degrees in Applied Mathematics in 2015, 2018, and 2024, respectively.

Work

Between August-September 2014 and March-June 2015, he completed internship programs in Avionics at the Aerospace Technology Center, National Institute of Aeronautics and Space (LAPAN), located in Bogor, Indonesia. In 2021, LAPAN was integrated into the National Research and Innovation Agency (BRIN) and is now known as the Aeronautics and Space Research Organization (ORPA).

Since February 2022, he has been a faculty member in the Department of Electrical Engineering at Universitas Islam Lamongan (UNISLA), Lamongan, Indonesia. The department was originally under the Faculty of Engineering but has since been reorganized as part of the Faculty of Science and Technology (FST).
